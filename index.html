About Kingdom :

Colchis region, 6th century BC

In the luxurious palace of Colchis, Pollux our front-end developer, and Serverux our back-end developer are excited like children and bickering in the big majestic corridors.

The expedition has begun everyone, the ship has dropped anchor and everyone is getting ready on deck. We will miss our loved ones but after all we have enough bottles of wine to see them appear at our side every evening. After many adventures, the crew managed to reach Colchis where King Aeetes, guardian of the golden fleece, welcomed us.

The king, who did not think that the crew would arrive alive, did not receive us with joy and challenged us to recover the fleece.
Aeetes has always dreamed of having the best website of all Greek empires to promote the kingdom and thus find a suitor for his daughter, Medea (which is not an easy task, she is a bit weird...).
